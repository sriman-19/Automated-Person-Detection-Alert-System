Face Recognition Tips

Our AI-powered system extracts facial features from the uploaded photo. For optimal recognition:

Do:
  • Use high-quality images
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Choose photos with a clear view of the face
  • Upload recent photos if possible
Don't:
  • Use blurry or pixelated images
  • Upload images with faces at extreme angles
  • Use heavily filtered or edited photos
  • Choose photos with multiple people
